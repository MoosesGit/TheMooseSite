About Me

My name is Mustafa Abdulhussein, but feel free to call me Moose. I'm currently in my junior year at the University of Michigan-Dearborn, studying Computer Science.

I've always had a passion for computers since I was a kid. I built my first computer at the age of 12, failing many times in the process. After countless attempts, I finally succeeded in building my first functioning computer. Little did I know, that would be the first of many.

After building my first computer, I discovered a game called RuneScape, which I quickly fell in love with. It was through RuneScape that I developed a passion for scripting, sparking my programming journey and solidifying my love for creating and problem-solving.

When I'm not programming, you'll often find me doing one of two things: reading or working out. Reading allows me to discover new ideas, gain fresh perspectives, and spark creativity, while working out clears my mind, relieves stress, and keeps me energized. Spending time away from the screen assists with my productivity, allowing me to be better equipt when facing complicated tasks.
